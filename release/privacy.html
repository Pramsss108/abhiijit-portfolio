Privacy & Evidence

Clear proof. Careful handling.

This page explains what the portfolio shows, what it does not collect, and how public work evidence is handled.

Website data

This website does not currently use account registration, advertising cookies or automated contact tracking. Standard hosting logs may record technical details such as IP address, browser type and requested pages for security and reliability.

Contact

If you contact me by email or WhatsApp, I use the information you send only to reply and discuss the requested work or opportunity. I do not sell contact details.

Portfolio evidence

Public screenshots and metrics are shown to explain my work. Owned-page evidence may be shown directly. Client-sensitive material is redacted, summarised or kept private. A company logo indicates work, a role or a named project—not necessarily direct employment.

Accuracy & permissions

Metrics include their available source, role and time window. Where an exact date or private record cannot be published, the portfolio says so. A correction or removal request will be reviewed promptly.

Your request

To ask about your information or a displayed artifact, email growabhijit@gmail.com. Include the relevant page or screenshot so I can identify it correctly.
