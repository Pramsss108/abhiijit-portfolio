Clear Space & Min Size

Exclusion zone

Keep clear space equal to the cap-height of the monogram (x) on every side. Nothing — type, edges, other logos — enters this zone.

Minimum size

Below these thresholds, detail collapses. Use the favicon variant under 32px instead of the primary mark.

Primary · ≥48px
Mark · ≥32px
Favicon · 16–32px
Lockup min width: 180px · Print min: 12mm
03

Logo Do's & Don'ts

Use on deep, dark surfaces

Indigo & near-black backgrounds let the glow and lime brackets breathe.

Swap to mono on light

On pale stock use the indigo/violet mono mark to keep contrast crisp.

Knock out white on violet

On full-violet fills, drop the P to white so the monogram stays legible.

Don't recolor

Never swap the palette or place on clashing rainbow backgrounds.

Don't stretch or distort

Always scale uniformly. No squashing, skewing or warping the mark.

Don't add effects

No drop shadows, bevels, outlines or rotation beyond the built-in glow.
