Privacy & portfolio evidence.

This page explains what information the portfolio handles, how contact links work, and how project evidence is selected for public display.

Contact information

This website does not currently use a contact form. If you contact me through email, WhatsApp or LinkedIn, the information you send is handled by that service and used only to respond to your message, discuss relevant work, or continue a professional conversation.

Analytics and cookies

The portfolio does not currently install behavioral analytics, advertising pixels or session-replay tools. If measurement is added later, this policy will be updated before those tools go live, and the implementation will avoid sending message content or unnecessary personal information.

Portfolio evidence

Results shown here come from owned properties or permission-safe summaries of professional work. Major claims should state the relevant source, period and my role. Client-sensitive information, private URLs and personal identifiers are removed or redacted before public publication.

External services

The site links to third-party services such as WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Instagram and client websites. Their privacy practices apply after you follow those links.

Questions or correction requests

To ask what information I hold from a direct enquiry, request a correction, or report portfolio material that should be reviewed, email growabhijit@gmail.com.
